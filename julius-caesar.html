Who is Julius Caesar?

Julius Caesar(Gaius Julius Caesar) was a Roman dictator
and general.He lived from 100 BCE to 44 BCE. At the
age of 31, Julius had already fought many wars. He had
turned the Roman Republic into the Roman Empire.
He was the conqueror of Gaul, victor in the civil war
of 49 to 45 BCE.

Family Background

Julius Caesar was born to an aristocratic family
that could trace their bloodlines back to the founding
of Rome. His parents had enough money for
comfort, but they weren't rich by Roman standards.
His full name was Gaius Julius Caesar.
He was born in Subura, Rome. He had 5 children named Cleopatra, Augustus, Caesarion, Julia Caesaris, and Gaius Julius Caesar.(information could be false)
